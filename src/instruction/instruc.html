CHEM230L: Drug Doping Lab

Instructions

1. Choose the case from the dropdown as assigned by the instructor and click Start

2. Choose a solvent ratio by changing the amount of CH3CN

3. Notice the amount of water also changes

4. Click Run when you have chosen a ratio combination

5. The chromatogram will be drawn in real time. When it is complete, hover over the peaks to see more information

6. Download the graph for later reference

7. Select Try Again if you are unhappy with the results, or Finalize Method if you are happy with the results.

8. To finalize your method, select the best ratio out of the ones you've tested from the drop down menu and click Submit. Use the Back Arrow to return to the solvent ratio selection page if you aren't happy with your runs.

9. Once you have successfully identified the best ratio, click on each of the peaks of the graph to see the mass spectra

10. Explore the graph by hovering over it

11. Download the graph. When you are done, use the Back Arrow to return to the chromatogram of the best ratio

12. Click Next when you are done

13. Select compounds you want to see calibration chromatograms for and click Run

14. Explore the chromatograms by hovering over them

15. Click compare to see compare the calibration curve and best ratio chromatogram

16. Click Try Again to return to the compound selection and run more calibration trials. Once you have collected all the data you need, you may move on to the data analysis.
